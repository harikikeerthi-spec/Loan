Cookie Preferences

Effective Date: January 19, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit our website. They allow us to recognize you and remember your preferences during your visit and across multiple visits.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly. These enable navigation and access to secure areas.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website, including pages visited and time spent.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and choices to enhance your experience on our platform.
  • Targeting/Marketing Cookies: Track your browsing habits to display relevant advertisements and content.

3. How We Use Cookies

  • To remember login credentials and session information
  • To improve website performance and user experience
  • To analyze website traffic and user behavior
  • To personalize content and recommendations
  • To comply with legal and security requirements

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party service providers to place cookies on our website for analytics, advertising, and other purposes. These third parties have their own privacy policies and cookie practices.

5. Managing Your Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • See what cookies are stored
  • Delete cookies individually or in bulk
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Disable cookies entirely

Note: Disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.

6. Your Consent

By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies. If you do not consent, please adjust your browser settings accordingly.

7. Cookie Retention

Cookies are retained for varying periods depending on their type. Session cookies are deleted when you close your browser, while persistent cookies may remain for several months or years.
