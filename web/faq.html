Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers. Find everything you need to know about your global education loan.

Q What is the maximum loan amount available?

We offer loans up to 100% of your total cost of attendance, including tuition, living expenses, travel, and health insurance. The maximum limits depend on the university and country of study.

Q Do I need a co-applicant for the loan?

Yes, most student loans require a co-applicant (usually a parent or guardian) with a stable income source. However, for certain top-tier universities, we offer loans without a co-applicant or collateral.

Q What is the typical interest rate?

Interest rates vary based on the lender, your academic profile, and the university. Rates typically range from 8.5% to 12.5% per annum. Use our EMI calculator to estimate your payments.

Q How long does the approval process take?

With Atlys Education, you can get a conditional offer letter within 48 hours. The final sanction and disbursement usually take 7-10 working days after document verification.
